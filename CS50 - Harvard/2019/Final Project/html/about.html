A little bit about Equity

Equity is a web page to help people control their finances and, principally, try to help them avoid getting debt and grow their assets. This project was born from the biggest course of Computer Science of the world, the CS50, where the final task was to develop a web page.

Equity has a simple interface, where you can register, log in and start controlling your finances. When you are loged in, it show you how much cash you currently have, all the money you spent in this month and a table where you can see the month history of all transactions.

Now talking a little bit about the person behind all this project, I'm Murillo Stein, a 19 year old guy who lives in Brazil and studies Mechanical Engineering at Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC). Currently I'm working with robotics and starting my studies into Artificial Intelligence.
